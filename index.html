Our Story

"Some love stories are written in the stars, but ours was written over late-night conversations, shared laughter, and a quiet knowing that we had found something extraordinary."

What started as a chance meeting between two people from worlds apart became a love story neither of us could have predicted. From the very first conversation, there was a warmth, an ease, a sense of coming home that neither of us had felt before.

Through every season, every challenge, and every joy, we've grown together in ways that continue to amaze us. Now, surrounded by the people we love most, we're ready to begin the next chapter of our journey — together, forever.
